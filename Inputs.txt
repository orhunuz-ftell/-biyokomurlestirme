Inputs

PS: Particle size of the biomass
C: Carbon content of the biomass
H: Hydrogen content of the biomass
N: Nitrogen content of the biomass
O: Oxygen content of the biomass
Ash: Ash content of the biomass
V: Volatile matter content of the biomass
FC: Fixed carbon content of the biomass
Cel: Cellulose content of the biomass
Hem: Hemicellulose content of the biomass
Lig: Lignin content of the biomass
HT: Pyrolysis temperature
FR: Feed rate of the biomass into the reactor
GFR: Gas flow rate into the pyrolysis reactor



Outputs

char: Yield of biochar
oil: Yield of bio-oil
gas: Yield of gas
